Our Mission

Afghanistan deserves better data

Most development information for Afghanistan is scattered, outdated, or locked behind paywalls. Decisions are made without reliable data. Programmes are designed without practical tools.

ADI exists to change that — providing open, rigorous, and practical knowledge across every sector of Afghanistan's development landscape.

This is not a charity. It is a knowledge infrastructure project built on the belief that better data leads to better decisions, and better decisions build a stronger Afghanistan.

ADI was built from a simple observation: there is no open, reliable, data-driven platform covering Afghanistan's development challenges across all sectors.

Everything is scattered, outdated, or behind paywalls. Practitioners, researchers, and policymakers make critical decisions without the data they need.

ADI is the attempt to change that — one tool, one dataset, one policy brief at a time. Built in collaboration with researchers and practitioners who share the same mission.
